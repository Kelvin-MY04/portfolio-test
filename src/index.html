ABOUT ME

Naval Architect & Marine Engineer

Hello! I'm Seo Yeon Park, a dedicated Naval Architect with over 4 years of specialized experience in ship structural design and hydrodynamic analysis. My passion lies in creating sustainable maritime solutions that push the boundaries of efficiency and safety.

Currently based in Seoul, I bridge the gap between theoretical hydrodynamics and practical vessel construction. I have a proven track record of optimizing hull forms for reduced resistance and ensuring structural integrity under extreme sea states. I am driven by the challenge of innovative vessel design in an evolving global shipping landscape.
